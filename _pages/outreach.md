My science outreach work has spanned an eclectic mix of activities. I have given talks in schools, universities, museums, zoom rooms, and breweries; sat on educational conference panels; organised activity days for young families; and even collaborated on museum exhibits. 

I have a particular interest in engaging young people from socio-economic backgrounds that are less likely to attend higher education, in order to create a more diverse and inclusive geosciences community that offers opportunities to those from all backgrounds.

I am happy to be contacted with queries about outreach work - please find my email at the bottom of the page.
